## 4.10.3 Shankar monopole in $^3$He-A
Shankar proposed a texture:
$$\vec{\Omega}(\vec{r})=\frac{\vec{r}}{r}\cdot f(r)$$
where
$$f(\vec{r})=\begin{cases}
    2\pi & r=0\\
    0    & r=\infty.
\end{cases}$$
This texture is called **Shankar monopole**
